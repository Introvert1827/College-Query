The placement session for any company generally has 3 â€“ 4 rounds. The first round is the written test, consisting of quantitative aptitude, reasoning (logical ability), English (verbal ability) and computer science subjects (C, C++, Data Structures, Algorithms, DBMS, OS, Networks, etc.). For some companies, you might also have a coding round, which is sometimes taken as a separate round and sometimes is clubbed with the written test. Further rounds are interview rounds, the first two usually being the technical round and successive rounds being the HR round.

English (Verbal Ability):

  • Verbal ability is an important section. It contains different types of questions which are intended to judge student abilities in word power, sentence correction, spotting-errors-2 and verbal reasoning. So, to score good marks in verbal ability, the candidates need to have a good vocabulary and a strong command of English like in-depth knowledge of grammar, adjectives, tenses, articles and more.

  • Best Website : India bix

Reasoning (logical Ability)

  • This section is to judge student thinking ability and how a student evaluates any situation in a logical manner.

  • Its generally contain question like puzzles, number series , missing number , data sufficiency , assumption and Conclusions , Syllogism (deductive reasoning as distinct from induction e.g. all dogs are animals; all animals have four legs; therefore all dogs have four legs) etc.
