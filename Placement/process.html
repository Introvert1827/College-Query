1. Qualification

    • Every Company set some criteria before taking a written exam for example they only allow student whose overall percentage in college is 75% or above (example).

    • Some product based comapny like google even not take written round they directly call student for an interview on the basis of their resume (This is generally happen in On- campus placement).


  2. Written Round

    • This round is to judge your subjective knowledge, verbal and problem solving skill.

    • This exam contains questions from following section :

      • Aptitude

      • Verbal Ability and logical reasoning

      • Codding question

      • Computer Science subject and programming

  3. Interview

    • Two Technical round (vary from company to company)

    • One HR round (vary from company to company)

Note:
  • Some company take separate coding round while some clubbed the coding round with written round.
