How to Build a good Resume

  • Apply with a crisp and clear resume.

  • Don't write unnecessary thing.

  • If you are a competative programmer and your rating is good on codechef and codeforces ,so you can attach the link of your profile on your resume.(you can also attach your leetcode profile if you have 😁).

  • Mention your project that you made and Technolgy which you learn. (Also provide link of the repository where you store your code)

  • Your GitHub profile.

  • Hackathon and coding events in which you participate.

  • Working Experience : If you did any internship , mention what type of work you had provided by the organization and your contribution to the organization (what changes you made).

  • Most Important : skills

    Separate your skill by giving different subtitles like

    • Coding Languages : C, C++ ,Java,Python,JS etc

    • Core Subject: OS (Operating System)     CN(Computer Networks)     DBMS(Data Base Mangement System)     OOPS(Object Oriented Programming System)

    • Technology: AI     MERN     MEAN     Cloud     ML

    • DataBase: SQL Server, MongoDB, MySQL,

    • FrameWorks: Spring, Django

    • Tools and IDEs:VS Code,Eclipse,IntelliJ,pycharm etc.


  • Hobbies or Interest (Optional)
