Serviced Based Company

  • Salary per month : 25,000 - 30,000 (it may vary in future some company also give 35,000 to 40,000)

  • Companies : TCS , Infoysis,Capgemini,Accenture, Wipro ,Cognizant etc.

  • These companies are mass recruiters (select student in bulk).

  • Working hours : 10 - 12 hours.

  • You never get free lunch and snacks.

  • Luxirious life : NO

Product Based Company

  • Salary per month : 1 lakh - 1.5 lakh (some directi and startups pay more than this)

  • Companies : Amazon , Google , Microsoft , Facebook ,Apple , Intuit , Adobe , Netflix , Goldman Sachs , DeShaw , TowerResearch, CodeNation (one the highest base salary in India)etc.

  • These companies are not mass recruiters (select only skilled student).

  • Working hours : 8 - 10 hours.

  • Unlimited snacks , coffies and lunch.

  • Luxirious life : YES

  • Separate space for gym , playing game etc
Note :
  • The above article only giving a difference and not intended that services based company are bad.

  • Since its difficult to get land into a product based company and many student are not able to do it.

  • So for having a backup you can try on service based company as well.

  • "It's to better to have a job in service based rather than to being unemployee after B Tech.

  • You can also switch your job from service based to product based if you able to manage your job and preparation.
