Second Year Roadmap

Note :-Follow the below procedure only if you want to get into the software field.


I hope you follow the first year roadmap properly now its time to follow second year.

  • Since you all are now comfortable with basic understanding of programming language (C++ or Java) and some data Structure and algorithm.If you not do that then first finish that then you come in this section. Now it's time give some hands on development side.

  • Please note one thing development has no relation with what you learn so far, as it is a different game.

  • So there are different ways to do development,it's upto you what you like :
    • Web Development
      • front-End
      • back-End
      • full stack (both back-end and front-end)

    • Mobile App Development
      • ios development
      • Android development
      • hybrid

    • Machine Learning etc.

  • Choose any one try to become expert on that and after you are get familiar with it make some good project on that which you will later add on your resume.

  • Learn some tools like Git and GitHub which help you how you can store your project remotely.

  • Apart from don't this forget to practice question on DSA (Data Structure and Algorithm) side by side by giving some fixed hours to DSA problem on daily basis. Also try CP(Competative Programming) by giving live contest on different website like Codechef , Codeforces . Increase your rating on this website which definately help you in getting job in product based company, if you are 5 star or above on codechef OR candidate master or above in Codeforces then your resume will definately get shortlisted by comapany.
  • Check out the third year roadmap just after your second semester exam over.
