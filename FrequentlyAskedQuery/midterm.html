Mid-Term Examination

Whenever we give mid-term in college we always confuse about one thing is this marks add up to our final result? Some say "Yes" some replied "No" while other not sure about both of these.So here I am clearing all your doubt regarding mid-term


Is it Compulsary to give Mid-term Examination?

"YES"It is mandatory to give mid-term Exam untill and unless you have strong valid reason for skipping the exam.Since if you skip the exam without a valid reason So during the time of allocating internal marks teacher check your attendence in a class as well as in mid-term (some teacher check some not).So be on the safe zone its better to attend mid-term exam,getting low or high marks that depends on you but attendence criteria you have to take it seriously.

Marks added to your final result?

I am not sure about this but RGPV never add mid-term marks in final result of a student.They only add your theory paper marks,pratical marks(alloted by external during viva),internal marks(alloted when you submit practical copy to your subject teacher and viva(if teacher takes)).

Note: some teacher gives internal marks based on your mid-term marks and attendence.So make sure you have to atleast attend mid-term.

Schedule of Mid-term

In each semester two mid-term conducted by college.

Time duration : 1- 2 hours
Marks: 20 (Mid-term I)   and   50 (Mid-term II)
