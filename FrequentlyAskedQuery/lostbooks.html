What to do if Your Library Book Lost?

So I am one of those who lost his issued library book of college.

These are some of the following step that you can take when you lost your book.

  1. Search it everywhere home, ask from friends and teachers , visit those rooms or labs where you put your bags or book before it lost.

  2. and if you still not find it anywhere go to the library and tell the library teachers about the book lost. They genearally said wait for 2 or 3 weeks if someone find your book he/she definately will return to the library.

  3. After waiting for 2-3 weeks, ask from library teachers about the book if they say "No one comes here for returning your book" then you have to buy the same book from the market and submit to the library teacher then he/she will update in your account that you return the book that you issued.

What if I not return any library book ?

Ans:- After a month you get charge by Rs1 per day per book and this charge will goes till you return all the books and also after you graduate college staff will never give you T.C. until you clear all the dues or return books to the library.
